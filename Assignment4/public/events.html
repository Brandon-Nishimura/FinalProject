Events Throughout the Year

An Octive Higher, we are more than just a piano studio. WE ARE FAMILY. Throughout the year, we try to host activities that our students would enjoy!

Fall

During the fall, our students and their families look forward to our Halloween Festival.

Winter

During the winter, our studio hosts a Winter Wonderland for our students and their families.

Spring

In the spring, our studio celebrates Easter by having a giant easter egg hunt and egg dyeing event!

Summer

In the summer, our studio throws a Summer Fun Day, which is a giant outdoor picnic event with great food and fun games for people of all ages!
