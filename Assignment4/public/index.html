Here at My Piano Studio, we offer piano lessons to students of all ages and abilities.

At My Piano Store, students experience firsthand that music is a creative art. They learn to express themselves through their own music. We believe the both music reading skills and ear training skills are equally important. Students are able to learn in a fun and enjoyable environment!
